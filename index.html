Welcome to the Game of Mancala!

×

The goal of this game is to get the most marbles in your Mancala (aka store) located on each end of the board!

- Each player has 6 pits with 4 marbles each (represented by #s). On each end of the board, the Mancala (or store) collects your total # of marbles needed to win.

- The game moves counterclockwise!

- When it is your turn, click one of your pits and the marbles inside will "empty" and distribute across the following pits, 1 marble per pit.

- If your last marble lands in an empty pit, you will collect all marbles held in the other player'sdiagonal pit!

- The game ends when either Player 1 or Player 2 has emptied all of their pits, and the person with the most marbles in their Mancala WINS!

Player 1 is BLUE & Player 2 is PURPLE.
